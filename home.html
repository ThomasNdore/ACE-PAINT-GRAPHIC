FAQs

  • Question: What is the difference between ceramic coating and paint protection film?

    Answer: Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds with the vehicle's factory paint, providing a protective layer that enhances gloss and repels dirt and water. On the other hand, paint protection film (PPF) is a transparent film that offers physical protection against scratches, rock chips, and environmental damage. Both solutions provide different types of protection and can be combined for maximum safeguarding.

  • Question: What is the process for paint correction?

    Answer: Paint correction is a meticulous process that involves removing imperfections, such as swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation, from the vehicle's paint. Our skilled technicians use specialized tools and products to restore the paint's clarity and shine.

  • Question: How long does it take to complete a vinyl wrap installation?

    Answer: The time required for a vinyl wrap installation depends on the size of the vehicle and the complexity of the design. Generally, it takes one to three days to complete the process, ensuring a precise and professional result.
